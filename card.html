Dogs

There are more than 150 dog breeds, divided into 8 classes: sporting, hound, working, terrier, toy, non-sporting, herding, and miscellaneous.

      Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
      Average Lifespan: 10-13 years
      Average Reproduction: 5-6 Puppies
      Habitat: Everywhere

Dogs are often referred to as a "man's best friend" and are trained to smell for drugs, guns, bombs, etc. They are also trained to guide blind people and calm people who have social disorders (i.e. aspergers and autism).
